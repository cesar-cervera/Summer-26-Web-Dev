About Me

I am a graduate student in Computer Science & Engineering focused on machine learning, large language models, and responsible AI. My work emphasizes interpretable and human-centered AI systems, particularly in education, public health, and information integrity, with attention to robust system design and real-world deployment constraints. I am especially interested in how AI systems reflect social values and impact underrepresented communities. I am currently pursuing an M.S. in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Notre Dame, where I also serve as a teaching assistant for Introduction to Artificial Intelligence. I completed my undergraduate studies in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics at Holy Cross College.
